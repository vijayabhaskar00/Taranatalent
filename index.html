What we do Story about us

Rules and regulations

First Round: Individual Entry

  • Participants need to register with a $60 fee, by compiling all the details on the registration portal.
  • Participants need to record their voice, upload in youtube and send youtube video link to taranaauditions@gmail.com with subject as your Order ID provided in ticket by 20th September, 2017.
  • Indian Raga, Selected students from Tarana Talent Workshop (People who got an confirmation email) & Tarana series-1 (17) Finalists can purchase ticket for $30 and can directly participate in Skype Round.
  • All final round selected candidates of Tarana series-2 will get a direct second round entry to the next series of TARANA.
  • All the finalists will be presented with prestigious TARANA certificate.All the finalists will be presented with prestigious TARANA certificate.
  • It is up to participant’s sole interest to choose any song and can sing in any language not exceeding 3 minutes.
  • It is important for participants to take care of proper recording by eliminating disturbances. Make sure your recording has lyrics audible and your voice/sound is disturbance free to the maximum. Poor quality may disqualify your entry.
  • Karaoke Track should not be used for round one submissions. Tambura or some form of drone for sruthi can be used.
  • It is mandatory to introduce yourself prior to your recording video specifics to be provided name, age and your location.

Second Round : Skype round (Semi-Final)

  • Shortlisted participants will be informed email. They further have a second round through skype where the participant has to perform live in front of judges.
  • In case a participant is selected a detailed email will be sent out providing second round specifications including the time slots etc.
  • Under any circumstances, if the participant fails to be available on the given time slot, his/her participation will be disqualified without any prior intimation.
  • It is requested for the participants to have a good internet connectivity for the smooth function of audition.
  • Approximately 70 to 80 people will be shortlisted for second round.

Third round : Grand Finale

  • Shortlisted participants will be invited to a neutral venue for the Grand Finale and will be asked to perform live in front of judges.
  • Venue and other details will be shared via in-detail email to the participants once the second round results are announced.
  • Your expenses related to participation, travel, lodging & boarding are borne by the participants and organizers are not responsible for any expenses.
  • On the scale of talent, 40 people will be shortlisted to Grand Finale.

Wild Card Entry Rules: Conditions Apply (only applicable to participants who makes through for the skype round)

  • It is our responsibility to share the video on Youtube.com from our official channel. It is your responsibility to get maximum outreach to your video by sharing it via social media platforms & gaining momentum for the video.
  • Videos with maximum number of likes and momentum will be shortlisted by Organizers.
  • On average a maximum of 9 participants will be selected to finals from wild card entry.
